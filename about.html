About me

Hi, i'm Sietse Andringa

I grew up on a farm in the north of the Netherlands and ever since I was a kid I have been interested in computers and enjoyed the process of creating and fixing things. In my spare time I love to work with computers. Most of the time I spend on gaming, but I also like to do stuff like photo editing, fixing problems for friends and family, or just fiddling around and be creative.
After working as an operator in a factory for more than 15 years, I decided it was time for a change and point my arrows towards my passion: computers. As a result, I now pursue a career to become a Front-end Developer, which combines my interests in computer technology and creation. Besides computers I also have a passion for traveling, music, movies and cars.
