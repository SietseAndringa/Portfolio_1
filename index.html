About me

Hi, i'm Sietse Andringa

I grew up in the north of the Netherlands and ever since I was a kid I have been interested in computers. I enjoy the process of being creative and fixing things. During work and in my spare time I have always loved to work with computers. I spend a lot of time on gaming and surfing the internet, but I also like to do stuff like photo editing, fixing pc and network problems for friends and family, or just fiddling around and be creative. After working as an operator in a factory for 15 years, I decided it was time for a change and point my arrows towards my passion: computers. As a result, I now pursue a career to become a Front-end Developer, which combines my interests in computer technology and creation. Besides computers I also have a passion for traveling, music, movies and cars.
