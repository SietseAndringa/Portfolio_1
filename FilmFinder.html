Coen Brothers Film Finder

Who doesn't like a good movie? I certainly do! And the Coen Brothers are one of my favorite movie directors.

On the this webpage you can search for Coen Brother movies based on categories and/or search tag. Clicking on a movie will bring you to the IMDB page.

This was a study assignment in which the goal was to add/remove content to the DOM based on user input. It is built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It uses a local file to act as a small movie database.
