Nutrition Log

Back

This app allows you to track your macronutrients. The total amount per nutrient (carbs, protein and fat) is logged and shown in a graph. The total amount of calories is also calculated and shown.

I built this app during a Javascript course and it uses various ES2015 techniques, such as DOM manipulation, classes, promises, fetching, modules, etc. The focus of this project was therefore mainly on the functional part and less on the content and styling.

During the course I learned all the fundamental aspects of Javascript and used them to build this app. Building the app step by step was a very educational process and it was satisfying to see it becoming more and more functional and complete. Some concepts were quite difficult to understand, such as classes, asynchronous programming and handling (nested) arrays and objects.
